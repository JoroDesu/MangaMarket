About Us

At MangaMarket, we’re passionate about bringing the best of the manga world to your doorstep. We are a dedicated manga
store based exclusively in the Philippines, offering the latest and greatest manga collections, all available for online
ordering and delivery within the country.

As manga enthusiasts ourselves, we know how difficult it can be to find the manga you love, especially in the Philippines.

Ordering from international stores was often costly, with shipping fees and customs delays adding to the frustration.

We created MangaMarket to provide a one-stop shop for Filipino manga fans, offering reliable, accessible, and affordable manga
without the stress of endless searching. Our goal is to make the manga experience easier and more enjoyable for fans across the Philippines.
